Odoo POS Stock

Now guide your customers with bulk purchases!!

Odoo POS Stock module allows you to display product stock and selection of stock location at point of sale. You can also deny or allow order when a product is Out-of-Stock. Also, you can set the stock limit below which order will get denied and can select stock location for products at point of sale.

Keep your product stock in check now!!
Odoo POS stock is a perfect solution to take away all your worries related to your product stock. Now you can easily keep a check on your product stock and manage them accordingly. Also, you can keep your customers aware about the out of stock by displaying them a message. ALso, it helps customers to manage their bulk purchases.
Advantages
  • You can set the stock limit of the products below which order will be denied.
  • Allow or deny stock as per the stock availability.
  • Set a custom error message for out of stock products.
  • Allow customers to manage bulk purchasing of products.
  • Display out of stock message at product unavailability.
  • Select the stock location as per your wish.
Highlighted features
  • Set the stock limit of the products.
  • Select the stock location.
  • Display out of stock message.
  • Manage bulk purchases.
  • Allow/Deny the order for products out-of-stock.
  • Select ’Stock type’ to be displayed.

Detailed Features List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Odoo Pos Stocks

Display product stock on the Odoo website’s frontend

  • Odoo POS Stock module allows you to display product stock at point of sale.
  • You can even do the selection of stock location from the backend.

Display the out of stock message at product unavailability

  • You can easily display the out of stock message at the time of product unavailability.
  • This will help customers to manage the bulk purchases from your Odoo website.

Manage the product orders from the backend

  • The module allows you to allow or deny the order for products out-of-stock.
  • You can Select ’Stock type’ to be displayed on Odoo website.

Set the stock limit from the Odoo backend

  • The module allows you to set the stock limit from the Odoo backend.
  • A seller can set the stock limit below which order will be denied.
  • POS Session View
  • Backend View
